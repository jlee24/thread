Get your look, together










ABOUT

Without the financial freedom to shop at mainstream retail stores, low-income working women often spend several hours at multiple thrift shops in search of a particular clothing item. Even with this high effort, they frequently don't find what they're looking for.

Our mission is to connect low-income shoppers to their desired look in less time by pooling together the browsing capacities of other thrift shoppers in the area.



Seek

Seek the clothes you want by requesting them

Spot

Spot others' desired clothes while you browse and shop

Browse

Browse community spottings of the clothes you want
