ABOUT

Thread saves you valuable time and energy to help you find what you want at thrift shops. Using the collaborative power of local crowdsourcing, it provides an elegant platform to easily request and discover the clothes you want while giving back to your community.
Seek

Send out requests using points to find the clothes you want in less time

Spot

Spot and snap others' clothes and earn points while you browse

View

Discover community spottings of the items you want
